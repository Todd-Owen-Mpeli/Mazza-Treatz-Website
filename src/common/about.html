About Us

Where we are

Proudly presented in the heart of Swansea City Centre with two stores, Mazza Treats is ideally located to serve the thirst for authentic oriental produce which has been rising in the UK recently.

 Supplying more than 1000 of Oriental produce - fresh, frozen and dried, at Mazza Treats we make great efforts to research and provide the authentic ingredients, foods and hidden gems that are found in local aisles of supermarkets in the Far west & East, and make them available to everyone.

Mission Statement

We have an aim here at Mazza Treats:

 To make Oriental food produce, whether it'd be cooking ingredients, snacks, noodles or drinks, easily accesible to anyone in the UK and Europe. We want to make Oriental cooking fun, interesting and easy! Not a challenge!

Mazza Treats is a dedicated In-store supermarket providing customers:

Good quality, authentic Oriental produce at good value.
 Over 1000 different products from countries all over the Far East.
 A team which offers honest, caring and excellent customer service.
 Easy access to ingredients, recipe ideas and information about Oriental cuisine and culture.
We have a passion to deliver your order to you, well packaged, on time and in perfect condition.

With this, we hope that shopping with Mazza Treats will be an enjoyable experience!
