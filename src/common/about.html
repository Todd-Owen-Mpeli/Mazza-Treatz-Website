About Us

Proudly presented in the heart of Nottingham City Centre with two stores, Oriental Mart is ideally located to serve the thirst for authentic oriental produce which has been rising in the UK recently.

Supplying more than 3000 of Oriental produce - fresh, frozen and dried, at Oriental Mart we make great efforts to research and provide the authentic ingredients, foods and hidden gems that are found in local aisles of supermarkets in the Far East, and make them available to everyone. Specialising in Chinese, Korean, Japanese, Thai, Malaysian, Philippine, Taiwanese, Indonesian, Vietnamese and Singaporean cuisine.

Oriental Mart is a family run business and started off as an online shopping service (one of the first in the UK) in 2006, delivering Oriental goods to the whole of the UK. As the website grew bigger, there was a demand to open a shop which allows us to provide produce and a local delivery service to the local area. In February 2008, we opened our first store in the Hockley area of Nottingham, and with the opening of the second store on the Goldsmith Street, Nottingham city centre in October 2018, we are now the biggest Oriental Retailer in the Nottingham area.

Oriental Mart's diversity in produce from different countries has earnt rave reviews over the UK, with our website being recommended by Wagamama Nottingham Cookbook, The Hairy Bikers Asian Adventure Cookbook and leading Korean cooking website www.maangchi.com.
